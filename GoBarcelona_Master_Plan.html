Agentic AI Setup Guide (Gratis Workflow)

How to configure your automated editorial team using n8n and Claude.

1. n8n Orchestration

Install n8n (Host it for free on a local VPS or Home Lab). Set a Schedule Trigger to fire every 15 minutes.

2. The Relevance Agent

Feed headlines from Google Trends (ES) into Claude 3.5. Goal: Filter for relevance against our primary personas.

3. The Editorial Agent

Claude rewrites top stories into English with our "Young Professional" voice. Outputs JSON directly to supabase.from('news_articles').

4. Automated Engagement

New articles trigger a Resend Node to notify matching segments in your subscriber database.
